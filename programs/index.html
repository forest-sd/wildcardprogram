Our Programs

Find the one that fits your journey.

Discovery Workshop

Two-day intensive that helps you identify your unique combination of skills, interests, and potential career directions. Includes personality assessment, skills mapping, and pathway planning.

Creative Sprint

Four-week program focused on building a portfolio project in your area of interest. Weekly mentorship sessions, peer feedback, and a final presentation to industry professionals.

Career Pivot Bootcamp

Eight-week intensive for professionals changing careers. Skills translation, portfolio development, network building, and interview preparation for your target field.

Leadership Lab

Monthly sessions for emerging leaders focused on communication, conflict resolution, team dynamics, and strategic thinking through experiential exercises and real-world case studies.
