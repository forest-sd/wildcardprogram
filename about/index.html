About

For the people the system wasn't designed for.

Our Story

The Wildcard Program was founded on a simple observation: the people who change the world rarely follow conventional paths. They're the ones who zig when everyone else zags, who see connections others miss, who bring unexpected skills to unexpected situations. But traditional education and career development systems aren't designed for wildcards. They reward specialization over breadth, compliance over creativity, and credentials over capability. We built something different.
